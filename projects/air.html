Air Hockey Puck Detection

  • Tech Stack: Python, Camera, Raspberry Pi, OpenCV

In this project i was guiding one of my junior colleague for the detection of puck on air hockey table and for finding the tragtory of that puck. First of all we have decided to detect the colour of the puck then the center of the puck and then we have estimated the tragtory of the puck. In this project the proto type was developed on raspberry pi and then it was delivered from odroid board as the processing of raspberry pi was bery slow.
